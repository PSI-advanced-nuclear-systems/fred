# Background

During 2024 and 2025, the Advanced Nuclear Systems (ANS) group at the Paul Scherrer Institute (PSI) carried out a development campaign to extend the capabilities of FRED for modelling the irradiation and transient behaviour of metallic fuel in sodium-cooled fast reactors. A key objective of this effort was to benchmark FRED against the SAS4A/M-FUEL code developed at Argonne National Laboratory (ANL).

This work was performed by Daniel Timpano, Konstantin Mikityuk, and Antonio Jiménez-Carrascosa, with technical support from researchers at Argonne National Laboratory. The work resulted in the following publications:

Timpano, D. Metallic Fuel Behaviour Modeling for the European Sodium Fast Reactor. Master's Thesis, ETH Zurich, 2024.

D. Timpano, A. Karahan, N. Stauff, K. Mikityuk, and A. Jiménez-Carrascosa, “Metallic
Fuel Performance Analysis for the European Sodium Fast Reactor (ESFR-SIMPLE):
Analysis of metallic fuel performance using SAS4A/SASSYS-1 - MFUEL,” Argonne
National Laboratory (ANL), Lemont, IL (United States), 2024. doi: 10.2172/2371706.

Timpano, D., et al. "Fuel Performance Benchmark for the European Sodium Fast Reactor with Metallic Fuel." Proceedings of TopFuel 2025, 2025.

# Verification

The simulations presented in this example reproduce the base irradiation case reported by Timpano et al. using the FRED-M-Na application.
